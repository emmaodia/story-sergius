Background & Education

SERGIUS OSEASOCHIE OGUN born 1963 attended Saint John Grammar School, Fugar, Edo State where he obtained West African School Certificate (WASC) in the year 1983. He studied Law at the University of Benin, Edo State and obtained Bachelors of Law Degree (LL.B) in the year 2003.

Leadership

is a member of the Federal House of Representatives representing the Esan North-East/Esan South- East Federal Constituency of Edo State under the platform of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP).

Experience

Prior to becoming a member of the Federal House Of Representative he had been in the oil & gas industry(upstream) since 1987 till date. This experience covers procurement and logistics manpower service, drilling services and rig hire. He is also currently managing director/ceo of a service company,

Sponsored Bills

Hon. Sergius Ogun has sponsored and voted on a number at the Federal House Of Representative. A Number of these bills are geared towards projects in his Esan North-East/Esan South- East Federal Constituency. Many others with a far reaching effect on the Nation.

Welfare

Urgent need to complete and commission the Northern Ishan Water Supply Scheme.(appropriated for in the 2016 budget).

Infracstructure

A resolution for the immediate establishment of an emergency response and ambulance bay by the National Emergency Management Agency (NEMA), at Ewu Hills, along the Benin-Abuja Federal Highway, Ewu, Edo State.
Need to compel the  Nigeria Civil Aviation Authority and other relevant regulatory authorities in the Aviation Sector to conduct routine checks on the state of Aircrafts operating within Nigeria and make public the reports.

Finance

Urgent Need to Investigate the activities of the Nigerian Oil industry regulatory authorities and the need to complete all ongoing and outstanding utilization processes of straddled oil and gas fields and for other matters connected therewith.
Need for Comprehensive Audit of All Sources of Revenue in Nigeria

Health

Need to Provide Welfare and Rehabilitation Centres for the Critically ill and Physically Challenged.

Security

Need to stem the serious flooding at Illushi and Ifeku Island communities in Esan South East Local Government Area of Edo State.

Policy

Need to mandate the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control to check and ensure that food and drinks prepared and served in hotels, fast food restaurants and any other restaurants meet globally accepted hygiene standard.
